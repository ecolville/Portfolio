Maps

My portfolio is a collection of various projects and assignments that I ahve completed at the University of Wisconsin-Madison and NASA Develop Program. I work across print and online mediums. My work focuses on maps and their relationship to visual storytelling.

Flow Map of bike route

This assignment required me to gather/generate raster and vector data. Then I resampled the DEM to generate a detailed hillshade with 3d Analyst. On top of that I laid a generalized landcover styled to be as realistic as possible in Photoshop. Finally, I used Illustrator to represent and explain the route.
